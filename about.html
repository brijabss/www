Hi, my name is Brianna Jablonowski!! I have loved dogs since forever and am very passionate about saving their lives.

Let me educate you on why you should adopt and not shop!!

All of my dogs growing up were shelter rescues and they are the most loving creatures.

For my birthday in 8th grade, instead of asking for gifts I asked for money to donate to the animal shelter. I also volunteered at one for awhile so they hold a special place in my heart.
